Why Fortune Consulting Exists

Most software is sold for revenue, not results.

I spent years watching companies bend their operations to fit off-the-shelf software. Then I started Fortune Consulting to fix it.

The problem I kept seeing

I worked as a technical specialist for WeWork's ecommerce systems, and before that in telecommunications. Different industries, same pattern: companies restructure their entire operations to fit the software they buy.

A new feature gets deployed. An API goes down. A vendor pushes an update. Suddenly, the business has to adapt — retrain staff, change processes, work around bugs. Every change creates room for error.

The software works. But the business bends to fit it, not the other way around.

Here is what I learned: most vendors are selling what makes them money, not what solves your problem. They sell licenses, subscriptions, add-ons. They are not incentivised to build exactly what you need — they are incentivised to sell you what they already have.

And when you buy off-the-shelf, you inherit complexity you do not need. Features you will never use. Workflows designed for someone else's business model.

What I do instead

Fortune Consulting exists to give you what vendors will not: honest advice about what you actually need.

Sometimes that is a simple Google Business Profile setup, not a R50,000 website. Sometimes it is automating one manual process instead of buying an entire SaaS platform. Sometimes it is building custom software from scratch with our partner BuildsByBuchanan — software that fits your business, not the other way around.

If you have been sold tools that do not quite fit, or you are tired of adapting your processes to software that was not built for you, let's talk.

I will tell you what you need, what you do not, and why. No jargon. No upselling. Just the truth.

Let's fix this together

Free 30-minute consultation. No sales pitch. Just an honest conversation about what your business actually needs.
