The Problem

A Cape Town-based plumbing service was getting zero inquiries from their website. They assumed they needed a complete rebuild (quoted at R12,000) and were considering shutting down their online presence.

Our Solution

After a free consultation, we identified the real issue: their Google Business Profile was unclaimed and unoptimized. We set up their profile properly, added service areas, optimized for local search, and created a WhatsApp Business account with automated responses.

Result

Within 2 weeks: 23 inquiries via Google, 14 via WhatsApp. First month generated R47,000 in new business. Client avoided wasting R12,000 on a website rebuild they did not need. Total investment: R1,800.
